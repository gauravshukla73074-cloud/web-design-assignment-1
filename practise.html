Some Travel Related Terms

1. Destination :
A destination is the place where a traveler chooses to go. It could be a city, country, island, or any location known for its attractions, culture, natural beauty, or experiences.
2. Itinerary :
An itinerary is a planned schedule of a trip that includes details like destinations to visit, activities to do, accommodation, and transportation arrangements. It helps travelers organize their journey efficiently.
3. Tour Package :
A tour package is a pre-arranged travel plan that includes transportation, accommodation, sightseeing, and sometimes meals and activities — all offered together at a set price, making travel easier and more convenient.
